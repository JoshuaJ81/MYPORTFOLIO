About Me

Since the Fall of 2018, I developed a love for designing and building web pages. At that time in my life, I worked with WP Bakery, a built in web page editor for WordPress websites. Fast forward to Fall of 2021, I decided I wanted a career in Front End Web Development.

I officially began my Front End Web Developer journey in January 2022, as I enrolled in Codecademy's Front End Web Developer course.

To date, I currently work primarily with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Below is my portfolio of projects I have built, starting from March of 2022. Enjoy, and thank you for viewing my Front End Web Developer portofolio.
